About Me

Hi there! My name is Christian Fin and I study Computer Science and Computer Engineering at UC-Boulder, but I am originally from Portland, OR. I am passionate about all areas of computer science and participate regularly in hackathons and coding competitions.

I also love sharing my CS knowledge with others- last summer I interned at the Oregon Musuem of Science and Industry, facilitating engineering challenges and teaching STEM concepts to visitors of all ages. Currently, I work for Coding with Kids as a computer science instructor.

Fun Fact!

From 1st through 8th grade I attended a french immersion school. I continued studying French in high school and sat for the DALF C2 exam my senior year. Being bilingual has opened so many doors for me and I try to make as much use of my French as possible.
