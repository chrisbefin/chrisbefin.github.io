Scrabble Helper

This was a group project created as the final assignment of CS2270- Data Structures. It is a program written in C++ designed to help you beat your friends in a game of Scrabble. You give it the tiles in your your hand and the tiles on the board that you can play off and it gives you the best possible words to play.
